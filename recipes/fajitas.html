Fajita Recipe.

Delicious juicy oven baked chicken fajitas

These fajitas combine spices such as paprika, cumin, oregano, and chilli powder to create a dish that your family will love!

Ingredients

  • 1 Green pepper
  • 1 Red pepper
  • 1 Yellow pepper
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 lb of chicken breast
  • 3 tbsp of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees farenheit
  2. Cut the peppers into strips
  3. Cut the onions into wedges
  4. Cut the chicken into strips
  5. Combine the peppers, onion, and chicken on a baking sheet
  6. Drizzle 3 tbsp of olive oil on top and dump the spices on as well
  7. Use a spatula to toss the chicken and vegetables until they are all covered
  8. Bake for 20 - 25 minutes or until the chicken is done
  9. Serve with warm tortillas, sour cream, and salsa!
